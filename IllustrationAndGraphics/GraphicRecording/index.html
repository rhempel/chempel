    Christine is able to synthesize key ideas from meetings, presentations, and conversations into images and text. The result is increased engagement and enables co-creation among participants.

    Graphic recording uses a combination of listening, drawing and design skills, graphic to capture contributions, connections and voices in real time.

    Christine uses graphic recording to translate insights into a collective vision of an event.

     

    The image below was recorded at a preliminary planning meeting for the McQuesten Urban Farm in Hamilton, Ontario. This drawing captures a collective vision and helped a neighbourhood group take their idea from abstract initiative to reality.
