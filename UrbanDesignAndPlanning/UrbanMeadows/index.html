    Urban Meadows

    Working with the community advocacy group “The Butterfly Movement”, Christine used a “Before”and After” illustration technique to create landscape plans and scenarios to encourage the conversion of underused municipal lawns to pollinator meadows in Vejle, Denmark.

    A new orchard and meadow plantings with mown paths adds beauty and productivity to the courtyards of this municipal housing project.

    Simple grasses and meadow flowers with mown paths turn an underused lawn of this civic waterworks building into a pollinator habitat.

    A small formal tapestry lawn adds aesthetic interest and increases biodiversity at this civic waterworks building.
