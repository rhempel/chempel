    Green Innovation Park - Uppsala

    Christine developed a master plan and preliminary architectural concepts for a new mixed-use research and innovation park at the Swedish University of Agricultural Sciences campus. The site is currently an abandoned veterinary hospital with asbestos contamination. The proposed projects will be constructed after demolition of existing structures but maintains some of the existing landscape form and most mature trees.

    Client: Akademiska Hus, Uppsala
